Residency Programme

Immersive Gurukul-Style Stay for Hindustani Classical Vocal Students at Radiance

Duration of Stay

  • Minimum stay: 7 days
  • Maximum stay: 30 days
  • Choose any duration in between based on your learning goals, practice intensity, and slot availability.
  • Extensions possible subject to availability.

Facilities Provided

  • Comfortable private or shared room with clean bed, fresh bedsheets, blankets, and pillows
  • Attached bathroom with hot water facility
  • 24×7 electricity and reliable high-speed WiFi
  • Dedicated study table and chair for theory/notes
  • Quiet, well-ventilated personal riyaaz (practice) corner with tanpura and shruti box access
  • Common areas for group learning and evening discussions

Food & Meals

  • NNutritious pure vegetarian meals prepared and served daily (breakfast, lunch, dinner + evening tea/snacks)
  • Children below 12 years receive fully cooked meals (including non-veg if requested in advance)
  • Adults preferring non-vegetarian food or special diets may use the shared kitchen for simple self-cooking
  • Filtered drinking water and basic kitchen utensils provided

Daily Schedule & Residency Guidelines

A disciplined yet joyful routine designed to maximise your musical growth:
  • Wake-up & guided group riyaaz: 5:00 A.M.
  • Lights out & complete silence: 9:30 P.M.
  • Mandatory participation in light daily chores (helps cultivate humility & discipline — a core value of the guru-shishya tradition)
  • Strict no-alcohol, no-smoking policy on premises
  • Maintain cleanliness and tidiness of your room and common areas
  • Yoga, walks, or gym are not formally provided but can be self-managed in the garden or nearby park

Residency Fees (All-Inclusive)

  • 7-Day Stay : ₹6,500
  • 30-Day Stay : ₹25,000 (best value)
  • Per-Day Rate : ₹1,000 (for custom durations between 8–29 days)
Security Deposit: ₹3,000
(fully refundable on the last day after room & facility inspection; deductions only for damages or rule violations)
