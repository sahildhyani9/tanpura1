About Me

Learning with Love and Laughter

With a deep-rooted passion for Hindustani classical music, I specialize in Dhrupad and voice culture, known for its profound depth and rich vocal tradition. Trained in the Dagarvani style, I have pursued formal education from Visva Bharati Santiniketan (B.A. & M.A.) and am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Indian classical music from Barkatullah University, Bhopal. My musical journey includes guidance under esteemed gurus like Shri Murli Dhar Jagudi, Ma Aloka Nandy, and Shri Niloy Ahsan, along with time spent at Ustad Nasir Moinuddin Dhrupad Sangeet Ashram and with the Gundecha Brothers. With over 7.5 years of teaching experience, I offer focus on developing voice, understanding Raag, and mastering rhythm through Dhrupad and Dhamaar.
