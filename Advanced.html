Advanced Level

Mastery-Level Course in Indian Classical Vocal Music (Hindustani Tradition)

What You’ll Learn

This elite level is exclusively designed for professional vocalists and highly trained singers who already command a strong foundation and performance experience. Here, the emphasis shifts from learning to refining, polishing, and perfecting—transforming good singing into unforgettable artistry under the guidance of a seasoned mentor.

Core Focus Areas

  • Tailored, One-on-One Vocal Coaching - Receive deeply personalised mentorship crafted around your unique voice, style, and artistic vision. Whether you sing Khayal, Dhrupad, Thumri, or fusion, every session targets your specific strengths and areas for elevation—be it breath mastery, micro-tonal precision, voice projection, or stage charisma.
  • Mastering Intricate Expression (Bhaav & Layakari) - Explore the finest nuances of Raag elaboration: extended slow and meditative Alaap-Jod-Jhala, complex Behlawa, multi-layered Taan combinations (Sapta, Sarvlag, Gamak, Koot, etc.), and sophisticated Bol Bant. Learn to evoke profound emotion (Rasa) through subtle Gamaka, Meend, and Andolan, making every phrase a masterpiece of feeling.
  • Advanced Bandish Presentation & Improvisation - Perfect the art of presenting Vilambit, Madhya, and Drut Bandishes with authority. Master rare and complex Taals (Ada Chautal, Deepchandi, Jat Taal, etc.), intricate Layakari (1½, 2½, 3½ beats), Tihais, and seamless Upaj (spontaneous creativity). Develop signature Gayaki elements that set you apart on stage
  • Performance Excellence & Artistic Identity - Intensive work on stage presence, microphone technique, concert programming, and audience connection. Prepare for professional recordings, competitions, or major concerts with mock performances, critical listening of legendary maestros, and detailed feedback on every aspect of your presentation.
  • Specialised Streams (Optional) - Choose deeper immersion in Dhrupad, Thumri-Dadra, Tarana, Rare Raags, or Musicology & Research as per your artistic goals.

Learning Approach & Support

  • Pure 1-on-1 Mentorship – No group classes; every minute is dedicated to your growth
  • In-Depth Recording Analysis – Professional recordings of your sessions with frame-by-frame feedback
  • Access to Rare Material – Archival recordings, handwritten notations of ustads, and advanced theory resources
  • Performance & Recording Opportunities – Guidance for concerts, albums, and collaborations

Class Frequency & Commitment

Advanced students are recommended one intensive session per week (60–90 minutes). The focused, high-quality mentorship format ensures maximum impact from each meeting, leaving ample space for personal riyaz, reflection, and artistic maturation. Serious students often supplement with daily guided practice material shared by the mentor.

Flexible scheduling available | Online & in-studio options | Sessions by prior appointment only
