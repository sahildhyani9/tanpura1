Online Class Policies & Guidelines

We want your musical journey to be smooth, joyful, and filled with steady progress. These policies help us maintain consistency, respect everyone‚Äôs time, and ensure the highest quality of learning.

Class Packages & Fee Payment

  • All students enrol on a monthly package basis (typically 8‚Äì12 classes per month depending on frequency chosen).
  • Fees are paid in full in advance by the 5th of every month.
  • Payments can be made via bank transfer, UPI, or the payment link shared by the academy.
  • Late payments may lead to temporary suspension of classes until cleared.

Rescheduling & Cancellation Rules

  • Maximum 2 reschedules allowed per month (this limit applies to both student-initiated and teacher-initiated rescheduling).
  • Any reschedule request must be made at least 24 hours in advance. Requests received with less than 24 hours‚Äô notice will be counted as a completed class (no makeup or refund).
  • Rescheduled classes must be completed within the same calendar month ‚Äì they cannot be carried forward.
  • In case of a genuine medical emergency (supported by a doctor‚Äôs note), one additional reschedule beyond the monthly limit will be granted.
  • If the teacher cancels or reschedules without prior notice, the missed class will be fully rescheduled and you will receive one complimentary class credit in the following month.
  • If the student misses a class without prior intimation, the session will be considered conducted (no reschedule or refund).

Vacations & Longer Breaks

  • Planned breaks of 2 weeks or more (holidays, travel, exams, etc.) must be informed at least 4‚Äì7 days in advance.
  • Fees for the month will be pro-rated accordingly (only if intimation is given on time).
  • Students may choose to pause their monthly package for a maximum of 2 months per year with prior notice.
  • Uninformed long absences will be treated as regular attended months.

Monthly Practice & Progress Tracking

  • Every student is required to submit at least one audio/video recording of their personal riyaz every month (via WhatsApp or Google Drive).
  • This recording should include scales, exercises, and any Bandish or Raag being learned.
  • The teacher will review these recordings and provide feedback during the monthly progress assessment session.

Fee Revision Policy

We deeply value long-term guru‚Äìshishya relationships and have structured our fee revisions to reward commitment and loyalty:
  • First year of learning: No fee increase.
  • Years 2, 3 & 4: A gentle, pre-announced increase once each year.
  • Years 5 & 6: Fees remain completely frozen ‚Äì no increase for two full years.
  • Year 7 onwards: The cycle restarts (same pattern of gradual increases followed by freezes).
This ensures that students who stay with us for many years enjoy significant stability and savings while the academy can continue delivering the highest quality of mentorship.

General Code of Conduct

  • Please join classes on time with a calm environment, good internet/device, and tuned tanpura/sruti app ready.
  • Recording of live classes is not permitted without prior consent.
  • Homework and practice assignments should be completed before the next class.
  • Respectful communication is expected at all times.
We‚Äôre truly excited to walk this musical path with you. These guidelines are here to protect your progress and our shared commitment to the art.
